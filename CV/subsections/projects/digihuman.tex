\subsection{DigiHuman}{Open Source Thesis Project, \href{https://github.com/Danial-Kord/DigiHuman}{project link}}{2022 - Now}
\begin{itemize}
    \item Created an open-source real-time \textbf{animation system} for 3D avatars, architecting robust \textbf{low-level} and \textbf{high-level animation code} and rigging pipelines bridging Unity3D, Python, and \textbf{Blender}.
    \item Enhanced spatial stability by fusing \textbf{BlazePose}, \textbf{FaceMesh}, and \textbf{MediaPipe} with low-pass and Kalman filters for robust facial/pose tracking.
    \item Architected a high-throughput processing pipeline handling 30 FPS motion data and integrated real-time \textbf{performance diagnostics} to identify and resolve render bottlenecks.
\end{itemize}
