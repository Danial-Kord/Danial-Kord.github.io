\subsection{PISE: Person Image Synthesis and Editing}{Academic Project, \href{https://github.com/Negiiiin/PISE}{project link}}{2023}
\begin{itemize}
    \item Reimplemented the CVPR 2021 paper "PISE: Person Image Synthesis and Editing with Decoupled GAN," and focused on human pose transfer challenges used in virtual reality and image editing.
    \item Used \textbf{PyTorch} and the DeepFashion In-shop Clothes Retrieval Benchmark dataset to replicate the results.
    % \item Addressed technical challenges such as pose mapping and occlusions by employing a two-stage generative process, and combined a human parsing map with the target pose for improved synthesis.
\end{itemize}
