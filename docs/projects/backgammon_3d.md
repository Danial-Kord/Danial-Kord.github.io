## Detailed Notes
- Built a turn-based multiplayer backgammon game with online play, offline support, and AI-driven decision making.
- The documented AI approach is Monte Carlo based, and the portfolio notes Photon as the online platform layer.
- This project is useful as an early example of board-game rules, networking, and gameplay AI in Unity.
