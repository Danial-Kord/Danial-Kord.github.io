# PISE: Person Image Synthesis and Editing

## Snapshot
- CV status: Active
- Timeframe: 2023
- Type: academic deep-learning reimplementation project

## Detailed Notes
- Reimplemented the CVPR 2021 PISE paper on person image synthesis and editing with decoupled GANs.
- Focused on pose transfer and image editing problems relevant to avatars, virtual humans, and appearance transfer.
- Used the DeepFashion In-shop Clothes Retrieval Benchmark and PyTorch to replicate published behavior.

## Tech and Systems
- PyTorch
- GANs
- Human pose transfer
- DeepFashion dataset
